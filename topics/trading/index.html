Welles Wilder's four indicators

24 Feb 2017, 15:53

welles_wilder / indicator / SAR / ADX / ATR / RSI / technical_analysis

1978, Welles Wilder published a book named New Concepts in Technical Trading Systems. In this book, he introduced four indicators to readers, including Average True Range, Relative Strength Index(RSI), Directional Movement Concept(ADX) and Parabolic SAR. Despite being developed many years ago, I am still surprised by their rigorous logics and practicality recent days. 1.

About me

My name is Jarry Wang.

I’m from China, lived in Shanghai during my childhood and lived in Hangzhou (Zhejiang), Foshan (Canton), Chengdu (Sichuan) and Taizhou (Zhejiang) for one or more years.

I’m currently living and studying in Cork, Ireland, incoming Software Developer this autumn. This is my Github page.

Photography Amateur

Maybe I’m not a qualified photographer, but I’m keen on taking photos. Although I usually take street snapshots, I’m also interested in Portrait, Food and Architecture.

My favorite photographers are Martin Parr, Vivian Maier, Jeff Wall, Marc Riboud, Ueda Shoji, Lv Nan, and Bernard Faucon. This is my Portfolio page. Recently I post my photos on Instagram.

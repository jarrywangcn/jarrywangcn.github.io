About

About me

My name is Jarry Wang.

I’m from China, born in 1994. I lived in Shanghai during my childhood, and studied German and finance in Zhejiang University.

I’m currently living and working in Canton, CN.

Forex Trader

As a self-employed forex trader, I’m working with two friends on a quantitative trading project called Cornucopia. I worked as a forex trader since March 2015, and focus on EURUSD and XAUUSD. After building on Cornucopia from Sep 2016, I started learning programming, mostly Python, and became interested in Computer Science.

Photography Amateur

Maybe I’m not a qualified photographer, but I’m keen on taking shots. I bought my first camera Sony Nex5 in April, 2014. After taking thousands of photos in these two years, I bought a Fuji x100s. Mostly I took street snapshots. But I’m also interested in Portrait, Food and Architecture.

My favorite photographers are: Martin Parr, Vivian Maier, Jeff Wall, Marc Riboud, Ueda Shoji, Lv Nan, Bernard Faucon.

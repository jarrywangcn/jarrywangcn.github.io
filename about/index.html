About me

My name is Jarry Wang.

I’m from China, born in 1994. I lived in Shanghai during my childhood, and studied German and finance in Zhejiang University.

I’m currently living and working in Chengdu, CN.

Photography Amateur

Maybe I’m not a qualified photographer, but I’m keen on taking photos. Although I usually take street snapshots, I’m also interested in Portrait, Food and Architecture.

My favorite photographers are Martin Parr, Vivian Maier, Jeff Wall, Marc Riboud, Ueda Shoji, Lv Nan, and Bernard Faucon. Here is my portfolio.
